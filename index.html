What Does The University of Iowa YDSA Do?

  • Educate the community about socialism and progressive politics.
  • Pressure the university to adopt progressive policy.
  • Make the case for political and economic democracy!

How Do I Get Involved In The UIowa YDSA?

  • Start attending meetings and events!
  • Follow us on Twitter and Instagram for updates.
  • Become a member of the national Democratic Socialists of America.

Where are Meetings?

We currently meet once a week in 215 Phillips Hall from 6:30PM to 7:30PM on Tuesdays during fall and spring semesters.
